Getting Started
================

.. |JAMA| replace:: *The Journal of the American Medical Association*

.. |jama| replace:: *JAMA*


For our exercise, we're going to select an article from |JAMA|.
We'll extract data about the authors,
the data they've made available,
and any published conflicts of interest.
This is a typical and useful scrape -
it goes a little deeper than a simple
article bibliography.

Let's start by simply looking at what |s|
looks like [#]_.
